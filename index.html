Analyzing Price Correlations in the Craft Beer Market

Understanding Craft Beer Pricing

Craft-Beer-AI utilizes advanced AI algorithms to analyze various factors influencing craft beer prices. Our goal is to provide brewers and consumers with insights into pricing trends and correlations.

Price Correlation Visualizations

Our AI analysis reveals interesting price correlations across different craft beer attributes.

Size vs Price Correlation

This scatter plot shows the relationship between beer container size and price. Generally, larger containers tend to have higher prices, but the correlation isn't always linear.

Country vs Price Correlation

This box plot demonstrates how craft beer prices vary across different countries of origin. It highlights median prices, price ranges, and outliers for each country.

ABV vs Price Correlation

This scatter plot illustrates the correlation between Alcohol By Volume (ABV) and price. Higher ABV beers tend to be more expensive, but there's significant variation.

Correlation Matrix

This heatmap shows the correlation coefficients between various factors affecting craft beer prices. Darker colors indicate stronger correlations, either positive or negative.

Key Findings

  • Beer size appears to have a significant impact on pricing.
  • Prices vary notably across different countries, possibly due to local market conditions and import costs.
  • There's a clear correlation between ABV and price, with higher alcohol content generally commanding higher prices.
  • The correlation matrix reveals complex relationships between multiple factors influencing craft beer pricing.

Conclusion

Our analysis demonstrates that craft beer pricing is influenced by a variety of factors including size, country of origin, and ABV. These insights can help brewers make informed pricing decisions and assist consumers in understanding the market.
