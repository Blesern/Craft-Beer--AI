Understanding Craft Beer Pricing

Craft-Beer-AI utilizes advanced AI algorithms to analyze various factors influencing craft beer prices. Our goal is to provide brewers and consumers with insights into pricing trends and correlations.

Key Price Correlations

  • Ingredient Quality: Higher quality ingredients strongly correlate with higher prices
  • ABV (Alcohol By Volume): Beers with higher ABV tend to be priced higher
  • Brand Reputation: Established craft breweries often command premium prices
  • Production Scale: Smaller batch sizes typically correlate with higher prices
