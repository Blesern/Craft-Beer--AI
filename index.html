Welcome to Craft-Beer-AI

Craft-Beer-AI is an innovative project that leverages artificial intelligence to analyze and predict trends in the craft beer industry. Our goal is to provide brewers, distributors, and enthusiasts with data-driven insights to make informed decisions.

Key Features

  • Sales trend analysis across different beer styles
  • Seasonal demand prediction
  • Flavor profile mapping based on consumer preferences
  • Geographical market analysis for craft breweries
